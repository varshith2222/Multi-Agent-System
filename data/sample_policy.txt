GDPR COMPLIANCE POLICY

SECTION 1: OVERVIEW
This policy outlines our commitment to protecting personal data in accordance with GDPR requirements.

SECTION 2: DATA PROTECTION PRINCIPLES
We adhere to the following principles:
- Lawfulness, fairness, and transparency
- Purpose limitation
- Data minimization
- Accuracy
- Storage limitation
- Integrity and confidentiality

SECTION 3: SECURITY MEASURES
All personal data must be protected using appropriate technical and organizational measures.

SECTION 4: DATA BREACH PROCEDURES
In case of a data breach:
1. Notify DPO within 24 hours
2. Assess risk to individuals
3. Report to supervisory authority if required

SECTION 5: COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
Regular audits will be conducted to ensure GDPR compliance.

For questions contact: compliance@company.com
